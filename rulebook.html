1. TOURNAMENT STRUCTURE

  • Tournaments may consist of Qualifiers, Invites, League Stage, Group Stage, Semi-Finals and Grand Finals.
  • The format, number of matches, map rotation and advancement criteria will be announced prior to the event.
  • GRIND TREAGER ESPORTS reserves the right to adjust match format due to operational or competitive requirements.

2. TEAM ELIGIBILITY & REGISTRATION

  • Each team must consist of four registered players.
  • Only one unregistered player may play per match (if permitted).
  • All players must use the registered IGN.
  • Fake tags, impersonation or identity manipulation leads to cancellation.
  • Smurf accounts are not allowed.
  • Maximum two substitutes allowed.
  • More than one unregistered player results in disqualification.
  • Changing IGN without informing management is prohibited.
  • Account sharing is strictly prohibited.

3. ENTRY & LOBBY RULES

  • Teams must join the custom room 2–5 minutes before match start.
  • Teams must sit according to the official slot list.
  • Only team name is allowed in room chat.
  • Failure to provide proof may result in zero points.
  • POV must be recorded and can be requested anytime.
  • No action will be taken without valid proof or evidence.
  • Only squad entry is allowed. Solo/Duo entries are prohibited.
  • Sitting in another team's slot may result in removal.
  • End-match screenshots must be provided when requested.
  • Management may request POV recordings anytime.
  • Allegations require proper evidence.
  • Videos/screenshots are required to support complaints.

4. DEVICE & PLATFORM REGULATIONS

  • Only mobile devices are allowed in mobile tournaments.
  • Tablets/iPads are not allowed unless stated otherwise.
  • Emulator usage is prohibited unless officially permitted.
  • VPN usage to manipulate region, ping or matchmaking is prohibited.
  • Minimum in-game level requirement may apply.

5. ANTI-CHEAT & COMPETITIVE INTEGRITY

  • Hacks, scripts, injectors, modded applications are strictly prohibited.
  • Third-party unfair advantage software is strictly prohibited.
  • Intentional feeding is strictly prohibited.
  • Sharing room ID/password externally is strictly prohibited.
  • Failure to comply may result in disqualification.
  • Exploiting in-game glitches is strictly prohibited.
  • Collusion between teams is strictly prohibited.
  • Ghosting via live streams is strictly prohibited.
  • Management may request live screen recording, device verification and account verification.
  • Confirmed cheating leads to immediate removal and possible permanent ban.

6. MATCH INTEGRITY & CONDUCT

  • Teaming between opponents is strictly prohibited.
  • Unsportsmanlike behavior is not tolerated.
  • Abuse towards players, staff or broadcast crew may result in penalties.
  • Prize money may be withheld in serious misconduct cases.
  • Management may apply penalty points if required.

7. TECHNICAL ISSUES POLICY

  • GRIND TREAGER ESPORTS is not responsible for device crashes, internet disconnections, power failures, battery issues or in-game bugs.
  • If a player disconnects, the match continues.
  • Rematches will only occur in verified server-wide failure cases.

8. PROTEST & APPEALS PROCEDURE

  • Protests must be submitted within 15 minutes of match completion.
  • Protest submission must include screenshot evidence, Match ID and Player IGN.
  • False protests may result in penalties.
  • Decisions made after review are final.
  • No public disputes will be entertained.

9. PRIZE DISTRIBUTION POLICY

  • Prize pool distribution timelines will be officially communicated.
  • Teams must complete verification requirements.
  • Disqualified teams forfeit prize eligibility.
  • Failure to comply with documentation or promotional obligations may delay or void prize distribution.
  • A team can win a maximum of 2 trophies per month.

10. MEDIA & BROADCASTING RIGHTS

  • By participating, teams and players grant GRIND TREAGER ESPORTS the right to broadcast matches live.
  • Management may record gameplay, use team/player names, logos and clips for promotional purposes.
  • Unauthorized rebroadcasting of official streams is prohibited.

11. PROMOTIONAL COMPLIANCE

  • Teams may be required to share official posts and tag official handles.
  • Teams may be asked to post trophy visuals and participate in promotional campaigns.
  • Failure to comply may result in prize hold or future participation restrictions.

12. PENALTIES & DISCIPLINARY ACTION

  • Penalties may include Warning, Point Deduction and Match Disqualification.
  • Tournament Disqualification and Prize Cancellation may be applied.
  • Temporary Suspension or Permanent Ban may be issued.
  • Repeat violations may escalate penalties.
  • Severity of action depends on violation level.
  • Management decisions regarding disciplinary actions are final.
  • Appeals do not guarantee reversal of penalties.

13. MANAGEMENT AUTHORITY

  • GRIND TREAGER ESPORTS reserves full authority to interpret and enforce all rules.
  • Management may modify rules before or during the tournament if required.
  • Management may disqualify teams, cancel prizes or ban teams from future events.
  • All management decisions are final and binding.
  • Management may withhold reasoning for disciplinary actions if necessary.
  • If any situation is not covered in this rulebook, official management decisions will apply.
  • Additional rules may be introduced for special events.

14. LEGAL & COMPLIANCE CLAUSE

  • Participation in any GT ESPORTS INDIA event constitutes agreement to all tournament rules.
  • Players must respect final decisions made by tournament management.
  • Defamation, misinformation or attempts to damage the organization's reputation are prohibited.
  • GT ESPORTS INDIA reserves the right to take action against misuse of tournament information.

FINAL DECLARATION

This rulebook applies to all GT ESPORTS INDIA tournaments, scrims and official competitive events unless otherwise specified. Participation confirms acceptance of all terms and conditions.
